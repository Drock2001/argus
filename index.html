We are argus.ai

Why argus.ai?

Increased surveillance is the need of the hour. Despite the strict guidelines given, it has been observed that people are not conferring to the rules (mob gatherings etc.) We intend to solve this problem using our AI bot.

How?

Our product makes use of CCTV footage to witness such incidents using deep learning and inform the concerned authority thereby fortifying the need for continuous automated surveillance and accelerating the plan of action, before such events go unnoticed and unruly. We wish to expand its use by detecting incidents such as accidents and informing the nearest hospital.
